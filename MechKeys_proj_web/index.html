Keyboard Specs?

"The Azio B68 features these Different Switches"

Cherry MX Blue provides a noticeable tactile bump and distinct audible click with each keypress. Requires about 50g of force to actuate with a total travel distance of 4.0mm. Ideal for typists who enjoy audible feedback and tactile responses.

Cherry MX Red delivers a smooth, uninterrupted keypress with no tactile bump or click. Requires about 45g of force to actuate with a total travel distance of 4.0mm. Known for quiet operation and ease of rapid keypresses.

Cherry MX Brown offers a subtle tactile bump without an audible click. Requires about 45g of force to actuate with a total travel distance of 4.0mm. Provides tactile feedback without excessive noise, making it suitable for both office and home environments.

Enjoy versatile connectivity with the Azio B68. It offers a reliable wired connection via a detachable USB-C cable, or switch to wireless using Bluetooth 5.0 or the 2.4GHz wireless mode with the provided dongle for a lag-free experience. Ideal for various setups and use cases.

Customize your keyboard with vibrant RGB backlighting, featuring 16.8 million colors and multiple lighting modes. Choose from static colors, breathing effects, reactive lighting, or wave patterns to match your style or create a dynamic atmosphere.

Built to last with an aircraft-grade aluminum frame, the Azio B68 combines durability with a sleek design. The double-shot PBT keycaps ensure longevity and resistance to wear. Its dimensions and weight are optimized for both stability and portability.

The Azio B68 supports N-Key Rollover, ensuring every keypress is registered accurately. It includes dedicated macro keys and media controls for enhanced functionality. For added comfort, it features adjustable feet and a wrist rest. Customize your experience further with the proprietary software for key assignments and lighting adjustments.
